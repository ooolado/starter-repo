# Anthropic Acceptable Use Policy (excerpt)

Anthropic's Acceptable Use Policy covers permitted and prohibited uses of Claude and other Anthropic services. Customers are responsible for ensuring that downstream users of Claude comply with the policy.

## Prohibited uses

The policy prohibits use of Claude to generate content that facilitates physical or psychological harm, sexual content involving minors, harassment, or unauthorized practice of regulated professions. It also restricts uses that would interfere with safety or moderation systems.

## High-risk uses

Certain domains - legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, election information - require additional safeguards including clear human oversight and disclosure that the response is AI-generated. Customers operating in these domains should configure prompts and filtering accordingly.

## Reporting

If you discover misuse, report it through the customer success channel. Anthropic reserves the right to suspend access to customers or end users who violate the policy.
